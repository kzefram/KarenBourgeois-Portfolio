About Me

I'm an ex-holistic Health Coach turned Full-stack and Software Quality Assurance Tester.

I have been homeschooling my daughter part-time since she was three, and that brings me to how I became interested in coding and software testing. She had approached me with a passing interest in coding. Of course, at the time, I didn't know much about it at all and needed to learn the basics.

I read many books, and within a month, I was online in classes and watched YouTube. I went with Mike Dane for most of my classes and MIT for the Scratch training; I thought it would be fun. As I was learning the HTML and CSS to teach her, and eventually a few more, I learned about animations and making things happen. "OOHH! That's cool! What else can I learn?" I then moved on to New Brunswick Community College for Manual Testing and UNB for a Full-Stack program.

My plans are extensive and challenging. Drop me a line to learn more about me and my goals.
